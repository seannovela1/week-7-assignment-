Course Reflection

What worked well over the last eight weeks and aided your learning process?

Over the last eight weeks, I found the structured lessons and the hands-on projects to be very beneficial. The consistent practice of coding and working through real-world problems helped reinforce my understanding of concepts. The weekly assignments and feedback were also very helpful, as they allowed me to identify areas for improvement.

What would you change about the course?

One change I would suggest is incorporating more interactive and collaborative activities. For example, group coding challenges or peer reviews could help reinforce learning and encourage collaboration. More real-world case studies or projects would also help make the course even more practical and engaging.

Did you learn what you expected to learn in this course?

Yes, the course met my expectations. I expected to learn more about web development, especially front-end technologies, and the course provided a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I feel more confident in my ability to build simple websites and understand how these technologies work together.

What concepts were new to you, and what concepts have you already encountered in the past?

Some of the concepts that were new to me included advanced CSS techniques, such as Flexbox and Grid, as well as JavaScript DOM manipulation. I had already encountered HTML and basic CSS, but the depth of knowledge I gained about layout techniques and responsive design was new and exciting. The introduction to JavaScript programming also gave me a deeper understanding of how web pages become interactive.
